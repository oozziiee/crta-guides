About the High Elf

Description:
Strong melee class with substantial magic ability. Can learn offensive and defensive spells.

Strengths:
Calling on the divine, they can deal damage to undead creatures. Buffing spells benefit teammates.

Weaknesses:
Limited to using blunt weapons.

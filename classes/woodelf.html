About the Wood Elf

Description:
A master of the bow, with some skills in melee. They can learn powerful bow attacks.

Strengths:
Can combine close in fighting with heavy ranged damage.

Weaknesses:
Close quarter fighting limits the benefits of their ranged weapons.

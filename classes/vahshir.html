About the Vah Shir

Description:
Focused more on dealing damage than avoiding it, the berserker is a powerful but reckless combatant.

Strengths:
Proficient with throwing axes.

Weaknesses:
Fighting style makes the berserker more vulnerable to attack than the other fighters.
